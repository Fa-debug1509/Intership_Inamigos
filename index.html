About Us

A Movement Rooted in Compassion

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit headquartered in Chhattisgarh, India. We serve across communities, working tirelessly for education, food security, animal welfare, women empowerment, and environmental sustainability.

"We believe in the power of collective action to create a more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society across India."
